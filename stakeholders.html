For Healthcare Providers

How We Help

Advantage Health Partners enables providers to thrive in value-based care while reducing administrative burden and improving patient outcomes. We serve as your operational partner, providing the tools, support, and expertise you need to succeed in risk-based contracts.

Reduced Administrative Burden

Our EHR-integrated workflows and dedicated care coordinators handle the documentation and follow-up, allowing you to focus on patient care.

Improved Risk Adjustment Scores

AI-assisted HCC coding and suspect identification ensure accurate documentation of patient conditions and appropriate reimbursement.

Enhanced Care Coordination

Our care coordination platform facilitates seamless communication and collaboration among providers, ensuring patients receive comprehensive care.

Access to Advanced Technology

Leverage our cutting-edge analytics, patient engagement tools, and clinical decision support systems without significant investment.

Financial Stability

Our shared savings arrangements and performance incentives provide a more predictable revenue stream and reward high-quality care.

The burden of documentation and reporting has been significantly reduced, and our patients are receiving better, more coordinated care. The financial stability we've gained has allowed us to invest in our practice and our people."

For Medicare Advantage Plans

How We Help

Advantage Health Partners serves as an extension of your team, driving improved quality metrics, enhanced member engagement, and optimized risk adjustment. We bridge the gap between your plan and providers, ensuring aligned incentives and collaborative care delivery.

Maximize Risk Adjustment Revenue

Our comprehensive approach to risk adjustment ensures accurate coding and documentation, optimizing RAF scores and appropriate reimbursement.

Increase STAR Ratings

Proactive quality improvement initiatives and gap closure strategies drive higher STAR ratings and bonus payments.

Reduce Total Cost of Care

By preventing unnecessary utilization and improving care coordination, we help lower medical costs and improve MLR.

Enhance Member Satisfaction

Our multi-modal engagement strategy improves member experience, driving higher retention rates and satisfaction scores.

Streamlined Provider Network Management

We facilitate collaboration with providers, ensuring alignment with your quality and cost objectives.

For Patients

Personalized Support

Preventive Care Focus
