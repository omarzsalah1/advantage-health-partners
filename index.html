The Challenge

Medicare Advantage plans face increasing pressure to reduce total cost of care, improve member engagement, and meet CMS quality requirements while balancing provider burnout and operational inefficiencies. Gaps in care persist, risk adjustment remains incomplete, and plans struggle to get providers fully engaged in value-based models.

Our Solution

Advantage Health Partners serves as the clearinghouse for gap identification and closure by leveraging advanced AI, deep provider integration, home health assessments, and multi-modal patient engagement. Our market-tested approach ensures that every stakeholder—from health plans to government agencies and providers—benefits from real-time, actionable insights.

Key Benefits

AI-Powered Care Gap Identification

Advanced analytics for real-time risk stratification and care gap closure.

Seamless EHR Integration & HIE Connectivity

Automated gap closure workflows inside provider EHRs and real-time bi-directional data exchange.

Multi-Modal Engagement Strategy

Combination of provider-based workflows, direct patient outreach, home visits, and telehealth to ensure compliance.

Provider Enablement & Training
