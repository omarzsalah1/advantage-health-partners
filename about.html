About Advantage Health Partners

Our Mission

Advantage Health Partners is committed to transforming value-based care through technology, interoperability, and care coordination. We act as the central hub for Medicare Advantage payers, providers, and public health agencies, providing market-proven solutions to improve member engagement and regulatory compliance.

We are dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare delivery in Hawaii by establishing a comprehensive, technology-driven, and culturally sensitive value-based care network. We aim to bridge the gap between providers and Medicare Advantage payers, improving patient outcomes, enhancing provider engagement, and achieving financial sustainability for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

We envision a healthcare system in Hawaii where:

  • Every patient receives coordinated, high-quality care tailored to their individual needs
  • Providers are empowered with the tools and resources they need to deliver optimal care
  • Health plans achieve their quality and cost objectives through collaborative partnerships
  • Healthcare data flows seamlessly across the entire ecosystem
  • The unique cultural and geographical characteristics of Hawaii are fully integrated into care delivery

Our Leadership Team

Our executive team includes nationally recognized healthcare leaders, data scientists, and population health experts who have successfully built, scaled, and implemented value-based care initiatives. With deep experience in both the national healthcare landscape and the specific needs of Hawaii, our team brings unparalleled expertise to drive meaningful transformation.
