Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for value-based care success in Hawaii

Care Coordination

Ensuring Every Patient Gets the Care They Need

The Problem
  • Provider burden - Overworked physicians and staff struggle to meet care coordination requirements
  • Missed quality measures - High-risk members fall through the cracks
  • Low patient compliance - Traditional outreach methods are insufficient
The AHP Solution
  • On-Site Care Coordinators: Embedded in clinics to ensure real-time gap closure
  • Home Health Assessments: Direct member outreach to capture missing diagnoses and ensure timely care
  • AI-Driven Patient Targeting: Stratifying members based on real-time claims, lab, and clinical data
  • Behavioral Science-Based Engagement: Multi-channel engagement that maximizes response rates

EHR Integration & Data Interoperability

Bridging the Information Gap

The Problem
  • Disconnected systems - Plans and providers operate in silos
  • Data fragmentation - HCC suspect lists and care gap reports are not actionable within provider workflows
The AHP Solution
  • EHR-Embedded Gap Closure Workflows: Automating real-time alerts, documentation, and provider decision support
  • HIE & Bi-Directional Data Exchange: Integrating data sources to create a unified patient record
  • Regulatory-Ready Data: Structuring data for CMS and HEDIS reporting compliance

AI-Driven Analytics

Precision in Value-Based Care

The Problem
  • Missed opportunities in risk adjustment - Providers struggle to identify high-risk, undocumented conditions
  • Low RAF accuracy - Plans cannot maximize reimbursements without reliable data
The AHP Solution
  • Predictive Risk Stratification: AI-driven models identify the most impactful interventions per patient
  • Automated HCC Capture: Ensuring accurate risk adjustment submissions
  • Quality Measure Optimization: Helping plans and providers meet HEDIS, STAR, and CMS compliance targets
